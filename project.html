National Court Portal

The National Court Portal is a comprehensive digital platform that revolutionizes the way our judicial system operates. As the developer of this project, I've designed and implemented a user-friendly portal that facilitates efficient case management, document tracking, and online court proceedings. This portal brings transparency, accessibility, and convenience to our legal processes, enabling users to interact with the court system seamlessly.

With the National Court Portal, legal professionals, litigants, and the public can access vital information, file documents, and participate in hearings remotely, making the justice system more inclusive and responsive to the needs of the modern world.
